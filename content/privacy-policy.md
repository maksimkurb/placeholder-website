# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: October 17, 2025**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Cubly ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services. These services are intended for private and internal usage only.

## Important Notice

**These services are designed for internal and private use.** By continuing to use our services, you acknowledge and accept that your personal information may be stored on Cubly's servers.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect and process the following types of personal information:
- Account information (name, email address, username)
- Authentication data (OAuth tokens, session information)
- Usage data (access logs, feature usage, timestamps)
- Technical information (IP addresses, browser type, device information)

### How We Collect Information
Information is collected through:
- Account registration and authentication processes
- OAuth integrations with third-party services
- Automatic logging of service usage
- User interactions with our applications

## How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
- Provide and maintain our internal services
- Authenticate and authorize access to applications
- Monitor and improve service performance
- Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
Your personal information is stored on Cubly's servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

### Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Your Rights

### Data Deletion
If you wish to delete all your personal data from our systems, please contact us at:
**privacy-requests@kurb.me**

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe and confirm when your data has been deleted.

### Other Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights regarding your personal data, including:
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability

## Third-Party Services

Our services may integrate with third-party applications and services. This privacy policy does not apply to third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

## Internal Use Only

These services are not intended for public use or commercial purposes. Access is restricted to authorized users for internal projects and development purposes only. Access requests are only accepted from close friends or family members.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
